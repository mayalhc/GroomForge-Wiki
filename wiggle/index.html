🔗 Wiggle 2 RTX x GroomForge PRO

"Beyond Creation, Towards Perfect Motion"

The creator of GroomForge PRO doesn't just stop at building the world’s best exporter. To ensure your masterpieces move as beautifully as they are built, we are also leading the heavy development of Wiggle 2: RTX Edition.

This is our commitment to providing a complete, high-end ecosystem for professional artists.

🛠️ Crafting the Future of Physics

We believe that a perfect pipeline requires both precision data and lifelike motion. That’s why we’ve dedicated ourselves to evolving Wiggle 2 RTX into a world-class physics solution.

  • A Unified Vision: Developed by the same mind behind GroomForge, ensuring the most seamless compatibility and optimized performance between your groom and your physics.
  • Pro-Artist Commitment: Wiggle 2 RTX is our "Service to the Community"—a powerful, versatile engine constantly updated to meet the rigorous demands of modern character production.
  • A Growing Ecosystem: We are continuously refining the RTX engine to handle everything from complex hair to dynamic tails and clothing, making it the ultimate companion to your GroomForge workflow.

Developer's Note:
"My goal has always been to solve the 'impossible' tasks for artists. By developing both GroomForge PRO and Wiggle 2 RTX, I want to give you total control over both the form and the soul of your characters. We build together, and we move together."

✨ Join the Evolution

Wiggle 2: RTX Edition is the result of our passion for perfect motion. Experience the physics engine designed by artists, for artists.
